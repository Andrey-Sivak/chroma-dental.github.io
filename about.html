Dr. Aksana Tkachenko

Dr. Askana Tkachenko has more than 15 years of practice experience and is dedicated to learn new techniques and employing state-of-the-art technology at her charming Chroma Dental office.

Dr. Tkachenko graduated from University of Western Ontario, Schulich School of Medicine and Dentistry as a Doctor of Dental Surgery, and has a Master degree in Dental Surgery from Minsk State Medical Institute in 1993. Continuing education is a top priority for Dr. Tkachenko. She has obtained many diplomas, awards and certificates

Dr. Aksana Tkachenko is an active member of the Ontario Dental Association, and constantly contributing her time and effort to the dental profession, the dental community or the oral health of Canadians.

Dr. Aksana Tkachenko believes in a personalized approach to dentistry. Relaxed 1-on-1 appointments will help you feel great about your smile and your health. When you visit with Dr.Tkachenko, you’ll appreciate her attention to detail, friendly and calming demeanor, and above all else, her commitment to your health and wellness.

We pride ourselves in providing outstanding care and building relationships. This, after all, has brought us most of our patients through word of mouth!

Beyond her work as a dentist, Dr. Tkachenko is a healthy-life activist, who loves sport, play chess, exploring new kitchen recipes and gadgets. She loves spend time with her family, going to museums, theatres as much as she enjoys gardening exotic plants and herbs at summer time.
